NO TIME TO DIE

Cast

No Time To Die is the 25th James Bond film to be made and the last one to star Daniel Craig as James Bond.

SYNOPSIS

Recruited to rescue a kidnapped scientist, globe-trotting spy James Bond finds himself hot on the trail of a mysterious villain, who is armed with a new technology.
